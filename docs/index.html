ðŸ“Š About 2G Networks

2G networks (GSM, GPRS, EDGE) provide very slow data speeds compared to modern 4G and 5G networks. When your device connects to a 2G network, you may experience:

  • Significantly slower internet speeds
  • Longer loading times
  • Reduced app performance

This app helps you know when you're connected to a 2G network so you're aware of these limitations.
