2. App Purpose and Limitations

Performance Monitoring Tool

Stingray Warning is designed to provide insights into your cellular network connection quality. The App:

  • Monitors cellular radio technology and carrier information
  • Provides alerts when network conditions may affect performance
  • Offers educational content about network technologies and performance

Monitoring Limitations

The App's monitoring capabilities are based on information available through iOS system APIs. The App:

  • Relies on iOS system APIs that have inherent limitations
