2. App Purpose and Limitations

Security Awareness Tool

Stingray Warning is designed to provide general security awareness regarding cellular network conditions. The App:

  • Monitors cellular radio technology and carrier information

Detection Limitations

The App's detection capabilities are limited compared to dedicated Software Defined Radio (SDR) hardware and professional security tools. The App:

  • Cannot detect all types of IMSI catchers or cellular surveillance devices

  • Cannot provide real-time analysis of radio frequency spectrum
